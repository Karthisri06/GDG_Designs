About Us


Professional member of the American Society of Interior Designers with more than
35 years experience in Industrial and Interior Design


Grant Design Group was established in February 1981. The firm provides high-quality, furnished interiors for residential and commercial projects. Commercial work, including retail, hospitality and office interior design are provided in collaboration with licensed architectural and engineering partners. Using AutoCAD and Autodesk 3D StudioMax, the firm also offers in-house professional design documents, renderings and virtual walk-through animations for its clients.

Anthony W. Grant, A.S.I.D, a professional member of the American Society of Interior Designers, maintains primary control over each project. He is NCIDQ certified and has served as a member of the Nevada Legislative Coalition on Interior Design; he has twice served as the Chairman of the Las Vegas Area of the American Society of Interior Designers.

Tony and his wife Nanette, a Principal of GDG, work together to create impressive, soothing environments that provide a respite for today’s busy families. Their professional and skillful attention to architectural detail, color, and scale, as well as their use of fine antiques, artwork, and other furnishings, helps them to develop their clients’ living spaces into a harmonious blend of both elegance and refined simplicity.

Mr. Grant and his wife, Nanette, are the parents of five daughters and one son.
